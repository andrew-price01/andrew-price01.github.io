About Me

I recently just graduated from Weber State University with a BS in Computer Science. Graduating from college has always been a long term goal of mine and am excited to have achieved it. Weber State offered an excellent CS program. I've been fortunate to have studied under some of the best professors Weber State has to offer.

Since graduating I have kept busy by extensively studying data structures and algorithms. One of my goals this year is to gain a better understanding of this topic. I have also been studying at PluralSight learning frontend framework technologies such as Angular. Between these two I hope to work on a few side projects, including some open sourced.

Some of my hobbies include playing chess, reading, lifting weights, and cooking! My current reading interests are philosophy and psychology. I enjoy staying active and go to the gym 3-4 times per week. Baking has also become a new favorite hobby of mine.
